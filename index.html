How to play Brain Quizzers...

  • Select your favorite category and click Play Game!
  • A question from your selected category will be displayed on the screen with 4 possible answers.
  • Select the answer you think is the correct answer.
  • After you selected your answer, you will see if your answer was correct or not.
  • For a good answer you will get a score of 10 points, for an incorrect answer, you will not get any points.
  • A new question will automatically be loaded until you have answered all the questions.
